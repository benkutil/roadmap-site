Dashboard

The Dashboard Tab allows you to create different views for different purposes, and then share those views with anyone (no login required). For example, create a view based on all your projects for resource forecasting / capacity planning purposes. Similarly, create a view based on 1 project, brand it with your logo, and share it with your client.
  • Views consist of a project universe and a set of widgets
  • Brand and share views with anyone (no login required)
  • Drag-and-drop widgets to customize your layout
  • Pick from more than 12 pre-built widgets, or create your own based on custom project attributes
  • Customize widget names, colors, and chart options
