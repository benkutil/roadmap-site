Profile - Mack Companies


Client Profile

Name: Mack Companies
Contact: Tyson Roth, Project Manager
Location: Tinley Park, IL (Chicago metro area)
Industry: Real Estate / Construction
Business Description: Real estate redevelopment and management
Demographics: 40 employees in the Chicago area

Client's Pain Point

Project management between 5 different departments for up to 500 properties at any given time.

Solution

Until Roadmap, we used multiple Google docs to track the progress of the properties through our process. Basecamp integration combined with custom project attributes tailored to each department has centralized our project management. Dashboards have been a valuable asset for our finance and accounting departments to see the overview while custom views and filters allows our 30+ users to view only the information relevant to him/her.

Benefits

  • Centralization of information
  • Customization by department
  • Clean reports and exports
  • Easy filtering
  • Basecamp integration
